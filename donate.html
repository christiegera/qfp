Impact

Where your money goes

We have no paid staff — 100% of financial donations go to food and operations.

Fresh food

Milk, eggs, and meat purchased fresh to include with nonperishable orders

Essential supplies

Stocking up on items that run low — bought at wholesale through Philabundance

Operations

Office supplies, phone service, and tools so volunteers and clients stay connected

Facility

Equipment maintenance and building improvements at our Borough complex space

Food Donations

Most needed items

All non-perishable food donations are welcome. Drop off during open hours (M/W/F 10 AM – 1 PM, T/Th 6 – 8 PM) at the back door — ring the bell and we'll meet you.

Canned Chicken Canned Ham (1–2 lbs) Pasta (any type) Canned Fruit (any type) Beets or Carrots Hamburger / Tuna Helper Peanut Butter (medium) Jelly (medium)
Please note: No glass jars (safety hazard). No out-of-date food items. No homemade or home-canned foods.
