Food Partners

Organizations that supply our food

In addition to our sponsors, we partner with regional and government programs to source food for our community:

  • Philabundance — Our regional food bank, providing wholesale food access
  • Bucks County Opportunity Council (BCOC) — Administers federal and state food programs
  • TEFAP — The Emergency Food Assistance Program (federal)
  • SFPP — State Food Purchase Program (Pennsylvania)
  • Giant, Walmart, & Target — Regular donated food pickups from local stores
