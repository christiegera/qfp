No Appointment Needed

We're here to help

If you or your family need food, come visit us during open hours. No judgment, no wait list — just neighbors helping neighbors.

How It Works

Your first visit

Getting started is simple. Here's what to expect when you come to the pantry for the first time:

1

Come during open hours

Monday, Wednesday, Friday from 10 AM – 1 PM, or Tuesday and Thursday from 6 – 8 PM. No appointment or referral needed.

2

Check in at the desk

Let the person at the front desk know you're new. They'll walk you through a short intake form — it's a federal/state requirement, not a test.

3

Bring ID

Bring a photo ID or proof that you live in the Quakertown Community School District (a utility bill works). You'll need this each visit.

4

Take home food for your family

We'll fill a food order based on your household size — targeting 3 meals a day for 3 days. You can visit once per week.

🏠

Who can use the pantry?

Anyone living in the Quakertown Community School District or other portions of Upper Bucks County who needs food assistance.

We serve households of up to 14 family members. The amount of food you receive is based on your household size.

📋

What you'll need

  • Photo ID (driver's license, state ID, etc.)
  • Proof of residency in the QCSD area (utility bill, lease, etc.)
  • Annual government affidavit of need (completed at the pantry)
Don't have the right documents yet? Come anyway — we can help sort it out and provide a pre-packed bag in the meantime.
📍

Live outside our area?

If you live outside the Quakertown School District, we'll help direct you to the nearest food pantry in your area. In the meantime, we'll send you home with a pre-packed bag of food along with bread and dessert so you don't leave empty-handed.

Where Our Food Comes From

Our food sources

  • TEFAP — The Emergency Food Assistance Program (federal government)
  • SFPP — State Food Purchase Program via the Bucks County Opportunity Council
  • Philabundance — Our regional food bank partner
  • Local stores — Regular pickups from Giant, Walmart, and Target
  • Community donations — Schools, businesses, churches, families, and individuals

Ready to visit?

Walk in during our open hours — Monday through Friday. No appointment, no referral, no judgment.
